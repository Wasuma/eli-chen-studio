Notion for Insurance Agents: Build Your Book of Business

Last updated: March 2026

The Insurance Agent's Challenge

Insurance is a commission-based business that runs on relationships and timing. You need to:

  • Track hundreds (or thousands) of clients and their policies
  • Remember renewal dates and proactively reach out for renewals
  • Manage commission income from multiple carriers and policies
  • Track leads from initial contact through to closed deals
  • Coordinate with a team (if you're an agency or broker)

Most agents juggle this in email, spreadsheets, and a CRM that was designed for car sales, not insurance. It doesn't fit, and deals slip through the cracks.

  • Organize your own way: Structure data around clients, policies, commissions, or teams—whatever makes sense for your business
  • Track everything in relationships: Link clients to policies to commissions. See the full picture instantly

Core Notion Databases for Insurance Agents

1. Client Master Database

Complete client profiles: name, contact info, preferred communication method, household income estimate, risk profile. Link to all policies they own. This is your single source of truth for each relationship.

2. Policy Database

Insurance policies linked to clients. Track coverage type (health, life, auto, home), carrier, annual premium, renewal date, current status, and commission earned. Color-code by policy type for quick scanning.

3. Commission Ledger

Every commission, when earned, from which policy. Auto-calculate totals by client, policy, agent, and date range. See which clients generate the most revenue.

4. Renewal Calendar

Automated list of upcoming renewals with client contact info, renewal date, current coverage, and last commission. Trigger 30-day and 60-day outreach reminders.

Real-World Insurance Agent Workflows

Scenario 1: Solo Agent Building First Book

You're a new independent agent. You have 100 clients across multiple carriers. Your Notion workspace:

  • Logs every new client and their policies
  • Calculates all commissions by carrier (to reconcile with commission statements)
  • Sets renewal reminders 30 days before each policy expires
  • Tracks conversion from prospect to closed deal

Outcome: You never miss a renewal, you track exactly how much you're earning, and you see which carriers are most profitable. By month 12, you're managing 300+ clients with zero confusion.

Scenario 2: Insurance Broker with 5 Agents

You're managing a team of insurance professionals. Your Notion workspace:

  • Shows each agent's book of business and earnings
  • Tracks lead sources and conversion rates by agent
  • Monitors which policies are up for renewal across the entire agency
  • Calculates splits and commission payouts automatically
  • Identifies high performers and areas for coaching

Outcome: Complete transparency into agency performance. You know who's closing deals, who needs support, and where your revenue is coming from.

Key Metrics You Can Track

Personal Performance

  • Total book size (annual premium across all clients)
  • Commission earned (by policy, carrier, or date)
  • Renewal rate (% of clients who renew)
  • New business closing rate (prospects to closed deals)
  • Average client lifetime value

Agency Performance (if managing agents)

  • Revenue by agent (which agents are most productive?)
  • Commission cost per policy (lowest cost to close)
  • Team retention (are agents staying?)
  • Lead source ROI (which marketing channels convert best?)

Handling Multiple Insurance Types

If you sell across multiple lines (health, life, auto, home, commercial), Notion's flexibility handles it:

  • Single unified database: All policies in one searchable database with "type" filter
  • Separate databases by type: If workflows are very different (e.g., health insurance is renewal-focused vs. life is more consultative)
  • Custom views: Show different agents only their policies, or show clients all their policies across types

Commission Structures in Notion

Most insurance commissions are:

  • Percentage-based: Health insurance is often 3-5% of annual premium. Set a formula in Notion to auto-calculate
  • Tiered: Earn more if you hit volume targets. Create conditional logic in Notion
  • Multi-agent splits: If you work with a partner, Notion can auto-split commissions and track each person's share

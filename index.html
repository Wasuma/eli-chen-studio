Digital Efficiency for Mental Health Professionals

Managing a therapeutic practice involves countless moving pieces: client notes, session schedules, treatment plans, billing, and documentation. Many practitioners still rely on scattered systems—some pen and paper, some spreadsheets, some disconnected apps. This fragmentation costs time and can impact the quality of client care.

At Eli Chen Studio, we create ready-to-use Notion templates specifically designed for mental health professionals. Our templates are built by someone who understands the actual workflow of therapists, counselors, and coaches—not just generic productivity templates adapted for your field.

Why Notion for therapy practices? Notion is free (or $10/month for teams), fully customizable, HIPAA-compliant-capable, works offline, and syncs across all your devices. It's the only tool you need for scheduling, notes, treatment plans, and client records.
